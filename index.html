School Library

Was Establised in 1992.

The school has a well equipped library with the latest publications. Students are encouraged to inculcate reading habits. Books ranging from fiction to non-fiction and from literature to science are available for both students and teachers. Newspaper, journals, magazines are available to stay updated with current affairs.

School Biology Lab

Was Establised in 1992.

Our Biology lab is equipped with biology lab tools and instruments. The lab has a well collection of plants and animal specimens used by our students for practical learning purposes. Various instruments like microscope, scalpel, slides etc available for students’ usage in the lab.
